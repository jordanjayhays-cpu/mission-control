## What to Include on the Page (Checklist)

- [ ] City/neighborhood selector (pre-select Chamberí or Salamanca)
- [ ] Therapist cards: photo, name, specialty, rating, Metro distance
- [ ] "How it works" in 3 steps with icons
- [ ] Pricing transparency: "Pagas al therapist. Nosotros cobramos 15%. Nothing upfront."
- [ ] FAQ: "Is my data safe?" / "What if I need to cancel?" / "Can I book same day?"
- [ ] CTA: email capture if not fully live yet → "Apúntame a la lista de espera"
- [ ] Footer: "Massage Pass — Madrid"

## What the Landing Page Must NOT Say

- Don't say "subscription" — the word makes people think they're locking in
- Don't compare yourself to Booksy or Treatwell — it makes you look like you're competing on their terms
- Don't promise a specific number of therapists until you have them
- Don't ask for payment info on the waitlist page
