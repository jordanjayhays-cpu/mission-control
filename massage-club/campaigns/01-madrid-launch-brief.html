Massage Pass — Madrid Launch Brief

Version 1.0 · May 11, 2026 · Pre-launch · First neighborhood: Chamberí

What This Actually Is

Massage Pass is a two-sided marketplace:

  • Supply side: Independent massage therapists who need more clients
  • Demand side: People who want massages but don't want to commit to a subscription

The problem for therapists: Booksy charges €29–99/month whether you get 1 client or 50. Most solo therapists have 4+ empty slots per week and can't afford to stay on Booksy for a listing they barely appear in.

The problem for clients: Google Maps shows you where massage places are — not who's available now. Walking in is a gamble.

The fix: One neighborhood at a time. 5 therapists, tight radius. We match availability with demand, take 15% only when a booking actually happens.

The Model — Micro-Launch (Weeks 1–3)

Goal: 5 studios + 10 clients in Chamberí. One neighborhood, tight radius.

  • Offer to studios: Free first month + 0% commission on first 5 bookings
  • Offer to clients: [X]% off first session or "4 sessions for price of 3" intro offer
  • Take rate: 15% per confirmed booking after free month
  • After Chamberí works: Expand to Salamanca, then Malasaña
