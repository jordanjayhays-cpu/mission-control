⚡ Your First Week with Apollo — Action Plan

Day 1
Set up Apollo account
Connect your Gmail. Configure email warmup. Set up your first campaign.
Day 2
Build first prospect list
Target: Event organizers at conferences in Madrid/Spain. Aim for 50 contacts. Use Apollo's filters: job title + location + company type.
Day 3
Write your outreach sequence
3 email variants (short/native, value-prop, question-based). Set up 4-step follow-up sequence (Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14).
Day 4
Launch your first campaign
Upload 50 prospects. Start sending 10-15 emails/day. Don't flood — drip.
Day 5
Check first replies, update CRM
Log every reply in Apollo CRM. Move hot leads to "Contacted" stage. Respond to anyone who replied.
Day 6-7
Review metrics, refine
Check open rates, reply rates. Kill low-performing email variants. Build your second prospect list (next 50).
