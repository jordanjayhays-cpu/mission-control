📊 Market Context

Bulgaria's 262,000 worker shortage explained:

• Demographics: Bulgaria lost ~600,000 people since 2012 (emigration). Population: 6.5M. Aging fast.
• EUR adoption Jan 2025: Stability boost. Investors flooding in. New factories + logistics hubs = more demand.
• Tourism boom: Sunny Beach (3M+ tourists/yr), Bansko ski resort, Burgas coastal. Burgas alone needed 18,400 seasonal workers Q3 2024.
• Construction surge: Infrastructure boom. Strabag + others building. Heavy demand for welders, electricians, machine operators.
• Meat processing: Major gap. Bulgarian meat industry needs foreign workers — local supply insufficient.
• Philippine angle: PISCO (Placewell) already works Bulgarian employers. Filipino workers in Bulgaria = growing trend.
