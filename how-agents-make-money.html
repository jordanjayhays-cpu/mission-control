⚙️ B2B Automation Agency

📊 Outcome-Based Pricing
$0.99 per resolution | $9.99 per qualified lead | $50-200/meeting booked
Charge per result, not per hour. Intercom Fin charges $0.99 per resolved conversation. Best alignment: vendor bears risk, client pays only for results. This is what Jordan should charge for neuro outreach.
🛠️ AI Agent Building (Freelance)
$100K in 6 months | $3K–$8K setup + $1K–$3K/month retainer
Build custom agents for businesses. $3K–$8K upfront setup + $1K–$3K/month maintenance. Clients own the agent, you maintain it. Contracts + milestones + partial payment upfront.
📦 AI SaaS Subscription
$99–$500/month per client | $150–200 ARPU
Build a product that runs itself. Vertical-specific agents (legal, healthcare, neurotech) show 3–5x higher retention. $29–$99/seat for SMB, $500+/month for enterprise.

🔑 What Fails

"Cost-plus pricing, copying consumer models ($20 ChatGPT doesn't work for specialized agents), and per-seat models that ignore agent autonomy."
  • AI wrapper chatbots with no clear workflow — $0
  • Generic "AI consulting" — saturated, low margin
  • Full autonomy without human gates — 1.2x ROI vs 4x ROI with human-in-loop
  • Per-token pricing with no buffer — API costs wipe margins
