📋 Design Decision Guide

Model A: Stable, predictable demand. Focus on margin% and profit/unit. Pick the option with the best balance of high margin + reasonable certainty.
Model B: Volatile, uncertain demand. Calculate Co vs Cu carefully. If Co > Cu (cost of overstock > missed profit) → prefer designs with lower total cost. Consider CV (coefficient of variation) if demand data allows.
